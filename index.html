About Me

I have been studying Environmental Geoscience for the last three years of my college career and have gained many skills from all the courses. My atmospheric science course has allowed me to develop simple tools using Python to analyze weather- related data. My GNSS course has provided me with the necessary knowledge to operate RTK gps, WASS gps, ArcGIS mapping program as well as Carlson GIS. I learned to survey small fields of land and worked on larger parks for my project. I'm able to collect way points, transects and transform them into the desired coordinate format. I excel at operating Excel, Word and other common programs throughout the years. I hope to acquire a career in the Resources Management field in the near future.

AGGIE SPENCE PARK LAKE PROPOSAL (8/2019 - 12/2019)

The project was about surveying and collecting data from the Aggie Spence Park. Our group aimed to determined the possibility of extreme flooding with the construction of a new lake within the park. We used RTK GPS to locate all the points of interests such as trees and bed creeks. We also collected transects all over the park in order to create a contour map that displays the elevations of the park.
